Submit to TRUEX

TRUEX verifies betting records using an append-only, lock-time enforced system. All submissions become public, immutable, and independently verifiable.

TRUEX is not a picks marketplace. It is a record verification engine.

1. Submission Format

Bets must be submitted in CSV format with the following required fields: 

username,sport,event,market,selection,odds,stake,timestamp_utc

Example: 

sampleuser,NFL,SEA vs NE,Spread,NE -5,-110,1.0,2025-10-01T17:05:00Z

2. Lock-Time Rules

All bets must be submitted before the official event start time.

Late submissions are automatically rejected. TRUEX does not allow backfilling or editing past events.

Once accepted, bets are permanently frozen.

3. Grading Process

Results are graded deterministically using official market outcomes.

  • No manual overrides
  • No retroactive corrections
  • No deletion of losing bets

4. What Becomes Public

The following information is published on your verified profile:

  • Total Units
  • ROI
  • Win Rate
  • CLV (if available)
  • Max Drawdown
  • Equity curve
  • Sport breakdown
  • PayloadTreeHash
  • SealSha256

5. Rejection Conditions

  • Missing required fields
  • Invalid timestamp format
  • Post-lock submissions
  • Malformed CSV
  • Duplicate entries

6. How to Submit

Email your CSV file to:

submissions@true-x.io

Submissions are processed in scheduled verification cycles. Approved bettors receive a public verified profile link.

7. Philosophy

TRUEX exists to separate verified performance from unverifiable claims.

If you want to prove your record publicly and permanently, this is the system.
