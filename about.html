About TRUEX

TRUEX is a public betting record verification engine. It exists to make “proof” normal.

TRUEX verifies records.
It does not sell picks. It does not run a feed. It does not allow edits.

What TRUEX is

  • Public verification for bettors who want credibility
  • Append-only logging — bets can be added, not edited
  • Lock-time enforced — no post-start submissions
  • Deterministic publishing — outputs are reproducible and sealed
  • Transparent artifacts — raw analytics JSON + verification metadata

What TRUEX is not

  • Not a picks marketplace
  • Not a social betting app
  • Not an influencer platform
  • Not a comment or ranking feed
  • Not a place to backfill wins

How verification works

  1. You submit bets in CSV format (units, odds, timestamp)
  2. TRUEX enforces lock-time rules (must be before event start)
  3. Accepted bets are frozen into an immutable record
  4. Analytics are computed and published with seal metadata
  5. Your verified profile is publicly viewable and shareable
What becomes public on your profile:
  • Total units, ROI, win rate
  • Max drawdown and equity curve
  • Sport breakdown
  • CLV (if available)
  • PayloadTreeHash + SealSha256 verification metadata
  • Link to raw analytics JSON
